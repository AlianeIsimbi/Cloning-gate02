About RSSB

Rwanda Social Security Board is committed to providing comprehensive social security services to all Rwandans, ensuring financial security and peace of mind.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, sustainable and accessible social security services that enhance the welfare and dignity of all Rwandans.

Our Vision

To be a world-class social security institution that ensures financial security and social protection for all.
